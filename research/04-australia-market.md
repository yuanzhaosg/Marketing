## The honest assessment (read before committing)

**Three real risks with AU-focus:**

1. **Monetizable ≠ defensible.** The tools that pay (Xero, MYOB, e-sign, Deel, Remote) are global/generic. The AU-native tools that'd give you a moat (Employment Hero, Deputy, Annature) mostly *don't* pay publishers. Your defensible content and your revenue come from different places — workable, but you must monetize accounting/EOR/e-sign while *ranking* on AU-regulatory depth.

2. **Volume ceiling.** AU search is ~1/10–1/13 of US. The playbook's $2–3K/mo target assumes cluster SV ≥300K — hard in AU alone. Realistic AU-only ceiling is likely lower, or slower, unless you go broad across accounting + EOR + e-sign.

3. **Thin anchor set in accounting.** Xero + MYOB dominate; there isn't a deep bench of "alternatives" tools to review. The EOR angle (Deel/Remote/Papaya) adds depth and the biggest bounties.

**Why it can still work:** Xero's 30% *recurring* is genuinely excellent, AU competition is far lower (DR<40 ranking realistic), the AU-regulatory moat is real, and the EOR keywords pay US$1,500/conversion while being AU-targeted. A focused 2-cluster site (AU accounting + hire-in-Australia) monetized on Xero+Deel+Remote is a coherent, rankable, defensible business.

**If revenue ceiling becomes the priority later:** the hybrid (US/global primary + `...in Australia` sub-pages) recaptures volume while keeping the AU moat. Easy to layer on once the AU core ranks.
