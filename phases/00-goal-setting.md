# Phase 0: Goal Setting & Revenue Math

> **Purpose**: Translate "$2,000–$3,000/month" into concrete, measurable keyword selection criteria. Every filtering decision in Phases 1–4 flows from these numbers.

---

## 0.1 The Revenue Math

Before picking a niche, understand what it takes to hit $2K/month:

### Three Monetization Models

| Model | Avg Commission/Sale | Conversion Rate | Sessions Needed for $2K/mo |
|-------|---------------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| SaaS Affiliate (recurring 20–30%, avg $150/mo tool) | ~$45 | 2% of affiliate clicks | ~22,000 sessions |
| High-ticket SaaS (recurring, avg $500+/mo tool) | ~$150 | 1% of affiliate clicks | ~13,000 sessions |
| Mixed (affiliate + lead gen) | ~$80 | 1.5% | ~17,000 sessions |

**Recommendation**: Target high-ticket SaaS niches (CPC ≥ $15). You need fewer sessions for the same revenue.

### Working Backwards from $2K/month

```
Target: $2,000/month by Month 12
Avg commission: $80/conversion
Required conversions: 25/month
Conversion rate: 1.5% of affiliate clicks
Required affiliate clicks: 1,667/month
CTR to affiliate links: 10% of sessions
Required sessions: 16,670/month

Traffic acquisition timeline (conservative):
  M3:  1,000–2,000 sessions
  M6:  5,000–8,000 sessions
  M9:  10,000–15,000 sessions
  M12: 15,000–22,000 sessions
```

## 0.2 Hard Selection Criteria

These thresholds are non-negotiable. Any niche that fails any one of these gets eliminated.

### Must-Have Criteria (ALL required)

```
✅ Cluster total addressable SV ≥ 300,000/month
   (You'll capture 5–8% = 15,000–24,000 sessions)

✅ ≥ 30 keywords with KD ≤ 25 in the cluster
   (New site can rank for these in M1–M6)

✅ Core keyword average CPC ≥ $8
   (Indicates commercial value and affiliate potential)

✅ ≥ 5 tools in the niche with recurring affiliate programs
   (Multiple revenue streams = stability)

✅ SERP top 10 has at least one site with DR < 40
   (Proof that small sites can rank)
```

### Instant Elimination Criteria (ANY one = eliminated)

```
❌ Cluster total SV < 150,000 → revenue ceiling too low
❌ Average CPC < $3 → not enough commercial value
❌ > 60% of tools have no affiliate program → can't monetize
❌ Top 5 SERP results trigger AI Overview with no clickable citations → traffic cut off
❌ No keywords with KD ≤ 20 → new site can't rank in year 1
❌ Google Trends 5-year decline → market shrinking
❌ G2 + Capterra + Trustpilot all in SERP top 3 → mega-aggregators block entry
```

## 0.4 Choosing Between 1 Site vs Multiple Sites

### Single Site (Recommended for Beginners)

**Do this if**: You're starting out, limited time, < $200/month tool budget.

- All DR authority concentrates in one domain
- Easier to maintain content quality
- Faster to hit first revenue milestone

### Multiple Sites (Monorepo Approach)

**Do this if**: You have validated the model, can produce 20+ articles/month, want diversification.

Benefits of a shared monorepo:
- Shared UI components (one design system)
- Shared SEO infrastructure (one codebase update = all sites updated)
- Lower marginal cost per additional site
- Protects against Google niche updates hitting one site

The three-site model that works:
- **Site A (Authority)**: Broad niche, high SV, aggressive content production
- **Site B (Commercial)**: Narrow niche, high CPC, fewer but higher-converting articles
- **Site C (Tool/Data)**: Calculator, comparison table, database — attracts natural links
